Mission Statement

The University of Waterloo NanoRobotics Group (UWNRG) is an undergraduate student team and research group that utilizes MEMS (micro-electro-mechanical systems) manipulation technology to explore technological marvels at the micro-scale. By encouraging collaboration amongst industrious undergraduate students and supportive professors, the group is able to generate novel ideas that showcase the utility and ingenuity of microrobots.
